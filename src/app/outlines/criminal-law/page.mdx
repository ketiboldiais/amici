# Criminal Law

_Disclaimer._ Criminal law is largely governed by state law. This outline, however, covers general principles of criminal law and cites to the Model Penal Code extensively. Not every state follows the Model Penal Code.

## Crimes Against the Person
### Simple Assault
- A person is guilty of simple assault if at least one of these conditions is met:[^mpc211(1)]
	1. the person purposely, knowingly, or recklessly caused bodily injury to another;
	2. the person attempted to cause bodily injury to another;
	3. the person negligently caused bodily injury to another with a deadly weapon; or
	4. the person attempted to put another in fear or imminent serious bodily injurty by physical menace.

[^mpc211(1)]: ~Model Penal Code~ ยง 211.1 (Assault).
