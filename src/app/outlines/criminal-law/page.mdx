## Crimes Against the Person
### Simple Assault
- A person is guilty of simple assault if at least one of these conditions is met:[^mpc211.1(1)]
	1. the person attempted to cause bodily injury to another;
	2. the person purposely, knowingly, or recklessly caused bodily injury to another;
	3. the person negligently caused bodily injury to another with a deadly weapon; or
	4. the person attempted to put another in fear or imminent serious bodily injurty by physical menace.

[^mpc211.1(1)]: _See_ ~Model Penal Code~ ยง 211.1(1).

### Aggravated Assault
- A person is guilty of aggravated assault if at least one of these conditions is met:[^mpc211.1(2)]
	1. the person attempted to cause bodily injury to another with extreme indifference to human life;
	2. the person purposely, knowingly, or recklessly caused bodily injury to another with extreme indifference to human life;
	3. the person attempted to cause bodily injury to another with a deadly weapon; or
	4. the person purposely or knowingly caused bodily injury to another with a deadly weapon.
